Did You Know? - Suggested Solutions Feature

  • Dena Sawyer
  • Mar 6, 2015

In this episode of "Did You Know Salesforce", we will talk about the Suggested Solutions Feature of Salesforce.

Your customer service department has reported that they are getting frequent support calls from customers for which the solution steps are the same. For each case, she has to type in the resolution steps. You are looking for a solution to make this more efficient.


The suggested solutions feature can display up to ten relevant solutions from the case detail page. It is invoked by clicking the View Suggested Solutions button on the case detail page. Alternatively users can search for solutions from the case detail page.
